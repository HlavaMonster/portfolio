My name is Dustin Hlava and I am a 27 year old front-end web developer currently living in Houston, TX.

I have a passion for writing clean, fast, and efficient HTML, CSS, and JavaScript using modern web programming techniques and tools. I have designed and developed numerous large web sites and applications, starting from idea through delivery. While I specialize and focus mainly on front-end coding, I have developed a broad web skill set which I am always eager to grow.

If you happen to catch me away from my computer, you'll probably find me skiing down a mountain, diving somwhere in the caribbean, working on my new house, or just enjoying dinner and a beer with friends.
